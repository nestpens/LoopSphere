Choosing the Right Aerpro Kit for Your Project

  1. Determine Vehicle Type & Usage
    Passenger cars & light trucks: APPS4R (wired rear) or APPS4WF (wireless front).
    Full-vehicle coverage: APPS4WFR for front and rear detection.
    Commercial trucks, trailers, caravans: APPS4WCR with optional LCD display.
  2. Assess Installation Constraints
    If routing cables through the firewall is impractical, opt for a wireless solution.
    For environments with extreme temperature or moisture, prioritize kits with IP67-rated ECUs.
  3. Select Desired Feedback Mode
    Audio-only beeper (rear kits) for simple alerts.
    Combined audio/visual LCD display for precise distance monitoring.
  4. Consider Future Expansion
    Wireless kits allow easy addition of extra sensors or LCD units without rewiring.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of parking sensor kits does Campad Electronics offer for cars and commercial vehicles?
Campad Electronics supplies a full range of Aerpro parking sensor kits, including wired rear kits (APPS4R), wireless front kits with LCD display (APPS4WF), wireless front-and-rear kits (APPS4WFR) and wireless rear kits for trucks, trailers and caravans (APPS4WCR). All are aftermarket parking sensors designed for easy DIY installation.
Are Aerpro parking sensors compatible with both plastic and metal bumpers?
Yes. Every Aerpro parking sensor kit from Campad Electronics is engineered to work with plastic and metal bumpers and many kits include under-mount brackets for specialised mounting options.
How does a parking aid system with both audible and visual alerts work?
The sensors emit ultrasonic pulses; when an obstacle is detected, the ECU triggers an audible beeper that increases in frequency and, for LCD-display kits, a visual bar-graph or numeric distance on the screen. This dual-feedback parking assist sensor gives you real-time awareness while reversing or manoeuvring.
Can I have parking sensors and a reverse camera fitted together?
Absolutely. Aerpro parking sensors can be installed alongside any aftermarket reverse camera. The combined “parking sensors and camera fitted” setup provides both visual (camera) and audible/visual (sensor) cues for a comprehensive parking sensor system.
What is the typical parking sensor price for a 4-sensor wired kit versus an 8-sensor wireless kit?
Pricing varies by model and retailer, but a standard 4-sensor wired kit (APPS4R) generally starts around AUD $229, while an 8-sensor wireless front-and-rear kit (APPS4WFR) is typically priced near AUD $649. Campad Electronics often runs promotions that can reduce the cost further.
Are Aerpro parking sensors considered universal parking sensors?
Aerpro kits are designed as universal parking sensor kits, meaning they fit a wide range of vehicle makes and models without the need for vehicle-specific adapters. The sensors are paintable and mounting brackets are adjustable to suit most bumper profiles.
How far can the wireless display communicate with the ECU in the APPS4WF and APPS4WFR kits?
Both wireless front kits (APPS4WF) and front-and-rear kits (APPS4WFR) use a 2.4 GHz link that supports up to a 30-metre range between the LCD display and the ECU, ensuring reliable signal even on larger vehicles.
Is a parking sensor relocation kit required for vehicles with non-standard bumper layouts?
If the original bumper mounting points are inaccessible, a parking sensor relocation kit (available separately) can be used to reposition the sensors while maintaining accurate detection zones. This kit is compatible with all Aerpro models sold by Campad Electronics.
Do I need professional installation or can I use self-install parking sensor kits?
All Aerpro kits are sold with comprehensive wiring diagrams and mounting hardware and can be a DIY parking sensor kits. While professional fitting is ideal, some owners successfully install the kits themselves using the supplied instructions.
What warranty and support does Campad Electronics provide for Aerpro parking sensors?
Every Aerpro parking sensor kit comes with an industry-leading 5-year warranty. Campad Electronics also offers technical support, installation advice and a “parking sensors supplied and fitted” service for customers who prefer a turnkey solution.
How do I find parking sensors near me or a local installer for Aerpro kits?
Use the “parking sensors near me” search on the Campad Electronics website or contact their nationwide service desk. They can direct you to authorized installers in your area or arrange direct delivery of the parking sensor kit to your location.
Are there any special features for the rear-only wireless kit (APPS4WCR) that benefit commercial vehicles?
The APPS4WCR includes a 60-metre wireless range, an adjustable beeper, IP67-rated waterproof ECU for external mounting and optional wireless LCD display (APPSDD). These features make it ideal for utes, trucks, vans, buses, trailers, caravans and horse floats, providing robust parking aid performance in harsh commercial environments.
